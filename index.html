What's Runebound?

Runebound is an elemental puzzle game with a touch on exploration.

You will have to discover and solve ingenious puzzles to uncover the mistery behind the downfall of an ancient civilization.

Features

    Four combinable elements: Place them anywhere and watch how they affect the world.
    A huge number of interesting and complex puzzles that will ignite a feeling of intrigue in you.
    Deep story that follows the clash between different factions of an ancient civilization.

About us

Obsidian Games is an indie video game developer that focuses on creating intuitive and exciting experiences for the players.
